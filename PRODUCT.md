# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Diners in and around Ancona (locals, couples, business lunches, tourists visiting the Conero coast) deciding tonight or this week where to eat. Context: mostly on a phone in the early evening, comparing osterias, looking for proof of quality before calling to book. Secondary audience: the restaurant owners themselves (Danilo, Marco, Matteo), who must feel the site is worth a premium fee.

The job to be done: convince a visitor within one scroll that La DegOsteria is the place, then get them to call +39 071 203031 or visit Via Pizzecolli 3.

## Product Purpose

Single-page marketing site for La DegOsteria, an osteria in the historic center of Ancona founded by three friends (Danilo, Marco, Matteo) around the idea of "riscoprire il piacere di stare insieme". Kitchen is dual-soul: terra (Fassona, ragù marchigiano, tagliata) and mare (Conero mussels, Adriatic clams, stoccafisso all'anconitana). Cellar is deeply Marchigian: Verdicchio, Passerina, Pecorino. Success = reservations (phone calls) and a perceived standard that justifies a 4.000€ build.

## Brand Personality

Convivial, artisanal, coastal. The warmth of a candlelit stone-walled osteria in Ancona's old town, not the stiffness of fine dining. Generous, proud of the Marche, slightly playful (Nutellamisù, DegO-Peanuts, "Gnocchi²"). Emotions to evoke: appetite, warmth, anticipation of an evening with people you love.

## Anti-references

- Black-and-gold "luxury fine dining" template with thin serif everywhere.
- Cream + terracotta + Playfair generic trattoria template.
- Stock-photo carousel sites with no point of view.
- Anything that reads as a pizza-delivery or TheFork listing page.

## Design Principles

1. Terra e mare: the dual soul of the kitchen (ember-warm land, current-cool sea) drives the visual world, including the 3D motion.
2. The menu is the proof: real dishes, real prices, typeset like they matter.
3. Motion serves appetite: every animation should feel like steam, embers, or sea current, never tech demo.
4. One scroll, one story: arrival, the three friends, the kitchen, the cellar, the booking.
5. Phone-first conversion: the number and hours are never more than one tap away.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast on all text. Full prefers-reduced-motion support (3D field becomes static, scroll reveals become simple fades). Semantic landmarks, keyboard-navigable menu tabs, alt text written in brand voice. Italian language (lang="it").
