Prep videos for the Terra & Mare flip cards.

  earth-prep.mp4   (a land / grill / meat-prep clip)
  sea-prep.mp4     (a fish / seafood-prep clip)

(the flip still works). The moment a valid .mp4 is present, it plays
automatically when the card is flipped.
